Features

Automated Credit Card Payments

Stay on top of your credit card payments with automated syncing between your Monzo account and designated pots. Set up rules to move money automatically based on your preferences.

Options include:

  • Transfer your full credit card balance to a payment pot
  • Transfer a fixed amount each month
  • Transfer a percentage of your available balance
  • Set daily automatic transfers or trigger on balance changes

"I use this to automatically move money to my credit card payment pot the moment my salary arrives. Never missed a payment since!" — Sarah K.

Smart Notifications

Get timely updates about your account activities directly in your Monzo app. Our smart notification system keeps you informed without overwhelming you.

Notification types:

  • Successful syncs and transfers
  • Payment reminders before due dates
  • Balance alerts when your credit card balance changes
  • Error notifications if something needs your attention

"The notifications are incredibly helpful. I always know exactly when money moves between my accounts." — Michael T.

Customizable Sync Rules

Create personalized rules that match your financial habits and preferences. Tailor when and how your money moves between accounts.

Customization options:

  • Choose which accounts and pots to sync
  • Set up multiple rules for different scenarios
  • Enable or disable rules as needed
  • Run rules manually or automatically

"I have different rules for my personal and joint accounts. Everything is organized exactly how I want it." — Emma L.

Complete Transaction History

Keep track of all your sync activities with a comprehensive history log. Monitor when and how much money has been moved between your accounts and pots.

History features:

  • View all past sync operations
  • Track successful and failed transfers
  • Search and filter by date, amount, and status
  • Export history for your records

"The history feature gives me peace of mind. I can see exactly what's happened and when." — James R.
