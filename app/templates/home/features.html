Features

Automatic Pot Syncing

Our main feature allows you to create rules that automatically sync your credit card balance to designated pots in your Monzo account. This helps you:

  • Keep track of your credit card spending
  • Automatically save money for upcoming credit card payments
  • Never miss a payment due to insufficient funds
  • Create a clear separation between your spending and saving

Rule-Based Automation

Create flexible rules that match your financial habits:

  • Transfer a percentage of your credit card balance
  • Transfer fixed amounts on a schedule
  • Transfer the full balance when it changes
  • Set triggers based on time or balance changes
  • Create multiple rules for different accounts
