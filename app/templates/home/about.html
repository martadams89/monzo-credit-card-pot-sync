About Monzo Credit Card Pot Sync

Our Story

Monzo Credit Card Pot Sync was created to solve a common problem faced by many Monzo users: keeping track of credit card payments and ensuring enough money is set aside for when payment is due.

The idea came from personal experience. As a Monzo user with several credit cards, I wanted an automated way to move money into my payment pot whenever my credit card balance changed. This would ensure I always had the right amount set aside for payments.

What started as a personal project has grown into a tool used by many Monzo customers to better manage their finances and ensure they never miss a credit card payment.

Our Mission

We believe in making financial management simpler and more automated. Our mission is to help Monzo users maintain better control over their credit card payments through smart automation that works seamlessly in the background.

Our Values

  • Simplicity - Financial tools should make life easier, not more complex
  • Security - Your financial data and privacy are our top priority
  • Transparency - Clear communication about how our service works
  • Community - Building features based on user feedback

How It Works

Monzo Credit Card Pot Sync connects to your Monzo account using the official Monzo API. It uses read access to monitor your credit card balances and write access to move money between your accounts and pots based on rules you define.

We use industry-standard OAuth authentication to connect to your account, which means we never see or store your Monzo login credentials. You can revoke our access at any time through the Monzo app or website.

The service runs entirely in the cloud, so there's no need to install anything on your devices, and your rules will continue to work even when you're not actively using the app.
