About Monzo Credit Card Pot Sync

Our Mission

Monzo Credit Card Pot Sync was created to solve a common problem: keeping track of credit card spending and ensuring you have enough money set aside for when the bill comes due.

Our mission is to help Monzo users manage their finances more effectively by automating the process of syncing credit card balances with savings pots, giving you peace of mind and better control over your money.

The Team

Our small team of developers and financial enthusiasts built this tool because we faced the same challenges in our own financial lives. We're dedicated to creating simple, effective solutions for personal finance management.

Privacy & Security

We take your financial privacy and security seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we never store sensitive financial information like card numbers or PINs.

Our connection to Monzo uses OAuth 2.0, ensuring that you never have to share your Monzo credentials with us directly.

For more information about how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.
