How Pot Mapping Works

When you map a pot to a credit card:

  • The app will sync money between your credit card and the selected pot
  • If your credit card balance increases, money will be added to the pot
  • The app will try to keep the pot balance equal to your credit card balance
  • You can change this mapping at any time
